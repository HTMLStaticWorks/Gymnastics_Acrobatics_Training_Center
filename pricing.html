Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing, enrollment, and training plans.

No hidden fees! Your monthly tuition covers all class instruction, use of gym equipment, and access to the Parent Dashboard. A one-time $25 registration fee applies to new students. We provide a list of optional personal equipment if you wish to purchase your own.
Yes. We require a 30-day notice for cancellations so we can manage class scheduling. You can also pause your enrollment for up to 2 months per year (e.g., for school exams or travel) without losing your spot in the class.
Absolutely! Families enrolling 2 or more children receive a 10% discount on the second child's tuition, and 15% on the third and beyond. Contact our admin team to apply this discount to your account.
We offer a make-up class policy: every student gets up to 2 make-up sessions per month. Simply log into the Parent Dashboard to book an available make-up slot at a convenient time. Make-ups must be used within the same month.
We recommend starting with a free 30-minute assessment session with one of our coaches. They will evaluate your child's current skill level, coordination, and enthusiasm to recommend the most suitable plan. Book your free assessment through the Contact page.

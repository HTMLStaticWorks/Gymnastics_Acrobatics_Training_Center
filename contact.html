Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you train?

We offer programs for all ages starting as early as 18 months in our parent-tot classes, all the way up to specialized adult gymnastics and elite competitive teenagers.

Do I need any prior gymnastics experience?

Not at all! We have foundational beginner classes designed specifically for athletes starting fresh. Our coaches ensure everyone progresses safely at their own comfortable pace.

What should my child wear to class?

For safety and mobility, we recommend a leotard or snug-fitting athletic wear (like a fitted t-shirt and athletic shorts). Please avoid clothing with zippers, buttons, or loose strings. Bare feet are required in the gym.

How do I schedule a make-up class?

If you miss a class, you can easily schedule a make-up session through your parent portal dashboard. We allow up to 2 make-up classes per month, subject to availability.

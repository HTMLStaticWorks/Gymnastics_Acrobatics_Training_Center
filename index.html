Skill Progression Framework

Watch your child grow through our clear, rewarding milestone system.

Level 1: Foundation

Focuses on basic rolls, balance, and spatial awareness.

Level 2: Intermediate Core

Introduces cartwheels, handstands, and bridge development.

Level 3: Advanced Skills

Mastering back handsprings, aerials, and complex bar routines.

Level 4: Elite Performance

Preparation for competitive events and high-level acrobatics.
